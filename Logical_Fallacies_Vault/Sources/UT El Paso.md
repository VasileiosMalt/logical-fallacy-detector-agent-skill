# UT El Paso - Master List of Logical Fallacies

A scholarly master list framing fallacies as 'junk cognition,' focused on identifying both contemporary and classic fallacies as used in modern discourse.

**URL:** https://utminers.utep.edu/omwilliamson/engl1311/fallacies.htm

**Coverage:**
- Over 100 named fallacies
- Contemporary and classic fallacies
- Focus on modern discourse

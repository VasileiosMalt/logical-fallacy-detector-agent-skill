# Affirming a disjunct

> **Category:** Propositional Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Formal

## Definition

Concluding that one disjunct of a logical disjunction must be false because the other disjunct is true; A or B; A, therefore not B.

## Also Known As

- Fallacy of the alternative disjunct
- False exclusionary disjunct

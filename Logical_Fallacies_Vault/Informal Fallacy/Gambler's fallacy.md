# Gambler's fallacy

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Statistical

## Definition

The belief that if deviations from expected behaviour are observed, future deviations in the opposite direction are more likely.

## Also Known As

- Monte Carlo fallacy
- Maturity of chances fallacy

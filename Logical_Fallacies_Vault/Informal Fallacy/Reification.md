# Reification

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Improper Premise

## Definition

Treating an abstract belief or hypothetical construct as if it were a concrete, real event or physical entity.

## Also Known As

- Concretism
- Hypostatization
- Fallacy of misplaced concreteness

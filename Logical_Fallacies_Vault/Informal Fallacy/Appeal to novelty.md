# Appeal to novelty

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

A proposal is claimed to be superior or better solely because it is new or modern.

## Also Known As

- Argumentum novitatis
- Argumentum ad antiquitatis

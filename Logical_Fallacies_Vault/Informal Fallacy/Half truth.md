# Half truth

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Improper Premise

## Definition

Telling the strict truth but deliberately minimizing or omitting important key details to falsify the larger picture.

## Also Known As

- Card stacking
- Stacking the deck
- Incomplete information

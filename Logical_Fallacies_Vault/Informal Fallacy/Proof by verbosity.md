# Proof by verbosity

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

Overwhelming an opponent with excessive detail or jargon to 'win' an argument.

## Also Known As

- Argumentum verbosium
- Gish gallop

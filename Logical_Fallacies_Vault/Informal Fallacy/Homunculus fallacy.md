# Homunculus fallacy

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Questionable Cause

## Definition

Using a 'middle-man' for explanation; explaining a concept in terms of the concept itself without explaining its real nature.

## Also Known As

- Homunculus argument

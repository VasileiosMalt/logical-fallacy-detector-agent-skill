# A priori argument

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Improper Premise

## Definition

Starting with a pre-set belief and then searching for any reasonable-sounding argument to rationalize, defend or justify it.

## Also Known As

- Rationalization
- Dogmatism
- Proof texting

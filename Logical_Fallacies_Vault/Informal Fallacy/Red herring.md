# Red herring

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

Introducing a second argument in response to the first argument that is irrelevant and draws attention away from the original topic.

## Also Known As

- Chewbacca defense
- Dead cat strategy

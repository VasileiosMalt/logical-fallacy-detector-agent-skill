# Ableism

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

Arguing that because someone is less capable than others, they naturally deserve less in life and may be freely victimized.

## Also Known As

- Con artist's fallacy
- Dacoit's fallacy
- Shearing the sheeple

# Appeal to mystery

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

Using the mysterious or unknown nature of a subject to avoid engaging with it or to support a claim.

## Also Known As

- Argumentum ad mysteriam
- Mystagogy

## Related Fallacies

- Appeal to ignorance
- [[Appeal to faith]]

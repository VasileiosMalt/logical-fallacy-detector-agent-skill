# Appeal to poverty

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

Supporting a conclusion because the arguer is poor (or refuting because the arguer is wealthy).

## Also Known As

- Argumentum ad Lazarum

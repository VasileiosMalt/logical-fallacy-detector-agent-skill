# False dilemma

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Improper Premise

## Definition

Two alternative statements are given as the only possible options when, in reality, there are more.

## Also Known As

- False dichotomy
- Fallacy of bifurcation
- Black-or-white fallacy
- Either-or fallacy
- False trilemma

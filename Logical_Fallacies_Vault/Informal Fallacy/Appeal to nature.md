# Appeal to nature

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

Judgment is based solely on whether the subject of judgment is 'natural' or 'unnatural'.

## Also Known As

- Naturalistic fallacy
- Biologizing
- Green fallacy

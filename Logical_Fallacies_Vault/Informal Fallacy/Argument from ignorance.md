# Argument from ignorance

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Relevance - Dismissal

## Definition

Assuming that a claim is true because it has not been or cannot be proven false, or vice versa.

## Also Known As

- Appeal to ignorance
- Argumentum ad ignorantiam

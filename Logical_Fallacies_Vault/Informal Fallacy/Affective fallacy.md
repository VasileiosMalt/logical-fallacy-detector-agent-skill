# Affective fallacy

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

One's emotions, urges or 'feelings' are innate and in every case self-validating, autonomous, and above any challenge.

## Also Known As

- Romantic fallacy
- Emotion over reflection
- Follow your heart

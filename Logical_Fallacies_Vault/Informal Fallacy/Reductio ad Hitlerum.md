# Reductio ad Hitlerum

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

Rejecting an argument because the person advancing it is associated with Hitler or the Nazis.

## Also Known As

- Hitler card
- Playing the Nazi card

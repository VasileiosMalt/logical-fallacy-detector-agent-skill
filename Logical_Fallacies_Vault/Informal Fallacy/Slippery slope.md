# Slippery slope

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Questionable Cause

## Definition

Asserting that a small first action will inevitably lead to a chain of related events resulting in significant negative consequences.

## Also Known As

- Thin edge of the wedge
- Camel's nose
- Domino fallacy

# Cherry picking

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Faulty Generalization

## Definition

Selecting individual cases or data that seem to confirm a particular position while ignoring a significant portion of related cases or data that may contradict that position.

## Also Known As

- Suppressed evidence
- Card stacking
- Incomplete information
- Half truth

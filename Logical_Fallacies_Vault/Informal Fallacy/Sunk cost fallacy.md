# Sunk cost fallacy

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Statistical

## Definition

Refusal to leave a situation because you have already put large amounts of time or effort into it.

## Also Known As

- Escalation of commitment
- Concorde fallacy

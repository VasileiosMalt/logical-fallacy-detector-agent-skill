# Argumentum ad baculum

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

An argument made through coercion or threats of force to support a position.

## Also Known As

- Appeal to the stick
- Appeal to force
- Appeal to threat
- Argument from the club

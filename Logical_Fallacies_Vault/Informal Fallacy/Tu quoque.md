# Tu quoque

> **Category:** Informal Fallacy
> **Subcategory:** Red Herring

## Definition

Claiming the argument is flawed by pointing out that the arguer is not acting consistently with the claims.

## Also Known As

- Appeal to hypocrisy
- You too
- Whataboutism
